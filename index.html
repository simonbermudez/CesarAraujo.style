Colour, considered

Cesar approaches every head of hair as a singular composition — reading tone, skin and light before a single foil is placed. His work is rooted in precision balayage and lived-in, dimensional colour that grows out beautifully and photographs even better.

Based at Vaffi Salon in Toronto and seen by appointment only, each session is unhurried, private and tailored entirely to you.
